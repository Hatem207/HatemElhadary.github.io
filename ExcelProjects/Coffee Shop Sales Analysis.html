Excel Project

Coffee Shop Sales
Analysis

Project Overview

This Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee shop sales data. It helps monitor sales performance, product profitability, customer purchasing patterns, and daily sales trends through interactive dashboards and key performance indicators (KPIs).

Orders Dashboard

Profit Dashboard

  • Pivot Tables
  • Pivot Charts
  • KPIs
  • Slicers

Business Problem

Coffee shop owners need a centralized reporting solution to analyze sales performance, identify top-selling products, evaluate profit by product, and understand customer purchasing behavior to support better business decisions.

Key Insights

Latte generated the highest sales among all beverages.
Coffee products contributed the largest share of total revenue.
Sales varied noticeably across different days of the week.
A small number of products generated a significant portion of total profit.
Interactive filtering enables quick comparison between products and sales trends.
