Power BI Project

Car Sales
Analysis

Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of car sales performance. It enables businesses to monitor revenue, sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators.

  • Pivot Tables
  • Pivot Charts
  • KPIs
  • DAX
  • Data Modeling
  • Slicers

Business Problem

Automotive companies require a centralized reporting solution to analyze sales performance, identify top-selling models, evaluate regional demand, and support strategic business decisions.

Key Insights

Identify top-performing car brands and models.
Compare sales performance across different regions.
Analyze monthly sales trends and seasonality.
Monitor revenue and profitability.
Support business decisions using interactive visual analytics.
